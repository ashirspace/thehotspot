Getting Started
What is thehotspot? ▾

thehotspot is an AI-powered outreach platform that automates lead generation, cold email writing, campaign sending, follow-ups, and analytics — all from a single dashboard. It connects to your Gmail account to send emails directly from your inbox.

How do I get started? ▾

Sign up with your email or Google account on the homepage. After logging in, complete your profile and connect your Gmail. From there you can import contacts, generate emails with the AI writer, and launch your first campaign in minutes.

Do I need technical knowledge to use it? ▾

Not at all. thehotspot is designed for business owners, founders, and sales teams — not developers. Everything from lead generation to sending campaigns is handled through a simple dashboard with no coding required.

Email & Outreach
Does thehotspot send emails from my real Gmail? ▾

Yes. thehotspot connects to your Gmail account via Google OAuth. All outreach emails are sent directly from your inbox, which means better deliverability, higher trust, and more replies compared to bulk email tools.

Are the emails personalised for each contact? ▾

Yes. The AI Email Writer generates a unique email for every single contact based on their company, industry, and category. No two emails are the same — which dramatically improves reply rates compared to copy-paste blasts.

How many emails can I send per day? ▾

thehotspot respects Gmail's sending limits and includes smart pacing to protect your inbox reputation. Currently the platform supports up to 50 emails per day with built-in warm-up logic to keep deliverability high.

Does it handle follow-ups automatically? ▾

Yes. The Follow-up Agent monitors your Gmail threads and sends up to 3 automated follow-ups (at Day 0, Day 3, and Day 7) to contacts who haven't responded. It stops automatically the moment a reply is detected.

Data & Privacy
Is my Gmail data safe? ▾

Yes. thehotspot only requests the minimum Gmail permissions required to send and read emails for follow-up detection. We never store your email content on our servers, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

Where is my contact data stored? ▾

Contact data is stored securely in Airtable, a cloud database. Your data is never shared with third parties or used to train any AI models. You can export or delete your data at any time by contacting us.

Billing & Plans
Is there a free plan? ▾

Yes. thehotspot offers a free tier so you can explore the platform, import contacts, and test the email writer before committing to a paid plan. Paid plans unlock higher send volumes, advanced analytics, and priority support.

Can I cancel anytime? ▾

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings, effective at the end of your current billing period.
