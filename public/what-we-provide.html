What We Provide
Every tool in one place
No duct tape. No third-party chaos. One platform, six modules, infinite scale.
Lead Generation
The Lead Agent scours the web in real time — finding brands, agencies, and companies that match your exact criteria and dropping them straight into your pipeline.
  • Searches by industry, region, and company size
  • Automatically deduplicates against existing contacts
  • Runs continuously — new leads every session
Cold Email Campaigns
Every contact gets a completely unique email — written by the AI Email Writer Agent, referencing their company, niche, and category. Never a blast, never a template.
  • Unique subject line and body per contact
  • Personalised with company name and category context
  • Warm-up pacing to protect Gmail deliverability
Follow-up Sequences
The Follow-up Agent monitors every thread. If a prospect hasn't replied in 3 days, it sends a nudge. Again at day 7. Stops the second they respond — zero over-sending.
  • 3-step drip: Day 0, Day 3, Day 7
  • Reply detection stops the sequence instantly
  • Fully automated, no manual scheduling
Campaign Analytics
Every email has an invisible open-tracking pixel and click-tracking on all links. The Analytics Agent surfaces what's working — by campaign, category, and subject line.
  • Real-time open and click tracking on every send
  • Campaign history with sent / failed breakdown
  • Reply rate and engagement trends over time
Contact Database
A structured contact manager built for outreach. Import, organise, and manage leads across five categories: Network, CPS, CPL, CPA, and Mobile — with full status tracking.
  • Five built-in categories with colour coding
  • Import from CSV or add manually
  • Status tracking: Pending, Sent, Replied, Closed
Gmail Integration
Emails go out from your real Gmail inbox — not a third-party domain. That means real sender trust, better deliverability, and replies that land directly in your inbox.
  • OAuth sign-in — no password required
  • Sends via Gmail API — your domain, your reputation
  • Daily warm-up limits protect your account health
