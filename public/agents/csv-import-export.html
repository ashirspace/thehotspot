What it does
Bulk import. Instant export.
Works with any spreadsheet app. Export from Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Nav, or anywhere else — import straight into thehotspot.
CSV Upload
Drag your CSV file into the uploader. The tool previews the first 20 rows so you can verify the data before importing.
Column Auto-Mapping
Supports common column headers: name, email, company, website, category, country, notes. Both lowercase and Title Case.
One-Click Export
Download all your contacts as a CSV instantly — formatted, clean, and ready to open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any tool.
Import Summary
After import, see exactly how many contacts were added and how many rows were skipped due to missing required fields.
How it works
Simple as 1-2-3
1
Choose Import or Export
The tool shows both options side by side. Click 'Choose CSV file' to import, or 'Download CSV' to export.
2
For import: preview first
After selecting your file, a preview table shows the first 20 rows. Review the data, then click the import button.
3
Done
Imported contacts appear immediately in your Contacts DB and CRM Lite. Exports download as a dated CSV file.
