What it does
Your outreach analytics, visualized
No more digging through logs. Every campaign you've sent is tracked and displayed in clean, scannable charts.
📈
Timeline Charts
Line charts showing opens and clicks over the last 14 days — see trends, identify spikes, and know which campaigns landed.
📊
Delivery Stats
Total sent, opens, clicks, and failed delivers — displayed as stat cards with percentage breakdowns at a glance.
📋
Campaign History
A full table of every campaign ever sent: name, status, sent count, failed count, and date — sorted newest first.
🔴
Status Badges
Campaigns are tagged as Completed, Running, or Pending — color coded so you can see the health of your pipeline instantly.
How it works
Simple as 1-2-3
1
Open Campaign Dashboard
No inputs needed — the dashboard automatically loads your data from thehotspot's database when you open the agent.
2
Read the charts
The timeline chart shows 14-day open and click trends. Stat cards show your overall numbers at the top.
3
Scroll to campaign history
Below the charts, a full table shows every campaign with its delivery status, sent count, and date.
