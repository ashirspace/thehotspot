What it does
Stop guessing which email works better
The agent evaluates subject lines, hooks, CTAs, tone, and length — and tells you exactly which version to send and why.
Clear Winner
No ambiguity — the agent declares Email A or Email B the winner and highlights it prominently at the top of the results.
Predicted Rates
Estimated open rate and reply rate for both variants, based on the quality of the subject line, hook, and overall copy strength.
Specific Reasoning
Detailed bullet points explaining why the winner works and exactly what the loser gets wrong — useful for rewriting, not just for picking.
One Improvement Tip
Even the winning email gets one concrete suggestion for how to make it even stronger before you hit send.
