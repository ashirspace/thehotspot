Personalized messages outperform blasts every time. thehotspot tailors every email to the recipient's industry and context — so it reads like you wrote it yourself.

Each email references the recipient's category, website signals, or business type — not just their first name. The Email Writer agent does this for hundreds of contacts in minutes.

  • Category-aware subject lines and openers

  • Your tone and brand voice baked in

Follow-ups that convert

Most replies come from the 2nd or 3rd touchpoint. The Follow-up Keeper sends timed, distinct follow-ups automatically — so you stop losing deals to silence.
