ACCESS

Know what is stored.

You can request a copy of personal data associated with your account, subject to identity verification.

CORRECTION

Fix account details.

If information is inaccurate or incomplete, contact us and include the account email that needs review.

DELETION

Request removal.

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data where applicable law allows.

01

How to make a request

Email privacy@thehotspot.in from the address connected to your account. Include the request type and enough detail to identify the relevant account.

02

Google data controls

You can revoke Google permissions directly from your Google account. Revocation may limit connected Gmail or profile features inside thehotspot.
