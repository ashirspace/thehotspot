This policy explains how we process personal data and support applicable rights requests.

1. Our commitment

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection requirements and supporting user rights.

2. Data we process

We process account, usage, contact, campaign, and integration data to provide the service.

3. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, or export of personal data.

4. Data transfers

Where data is processed by vendors, we use reasonable safeguards and limit use to product delivery.

5. Contact

Email privacy@thehotspot.in for privacy or data rights requests.
