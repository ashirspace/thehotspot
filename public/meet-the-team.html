Lead Scout

Discovery Agent

Combs the web and your connected data sources to surface qualified leads that match your ideal customer profile. No manual hunting, no stale lists.

  • Scrapes websites, directories, and social profiles
  • Filters by category, region, and engagement signals
  • Deduplicates against your existing contact database
  • Enriches leads with company size, website, and contact info
Web Scraping Data Enrichment Deduplication

Email Writer

Copywriting Agent

Crafts personalized cold emails for every contact based on their industry, website, and what your service can do for them — never generic, always on-brand.

  • Reads contact category and tailors the angle accordingly
  • Generates subject lines optimized for open rates
  • Uses your tone, voice, and proven templates
  • Queues drafts for review before sending
Personalization Template Engine A/B Testing

Outreach Sender

Delivery Agent

Sends emails through Gmail on your behalf, respecting daily send limits and timing windows to maximize deliverability and avoid spam filters.

  • Sends via your actual Gmail account (not a third-party domain)
  • Paces sends to stay within Gmail API limits
  • Randomizes send times to mimic human behavior
  • Logs every sent email to your contact's history
Gmail API Rate Limiting Send Scheduling

Follow-up Keeper

Persistence Agent

Automatically sends timed follow-up messages to contacts who haven't replied, then marks contacts as closed or escalates them based on response patterns.

  • Configurable follow-up delays (3 days, 7 days, 14 days)
  • Stops automatically if a reply is detected
  • Each follow-up has a distinct angle — not the same email twice
  • Updates contact status to Followed Up / Closed automatically
Drip Sequences Reply Detection Auto-close

Analytics Tracker

Insights Agent

Aggregates all outreach data into a live dashboard — tracks emails sent, reply rates by category, conversion trends over time, and surfaces which campaigns are working so you can double down on them.

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly send summaries
  • Reply rate by category and subject line
  • Tracks which follow-up sequence performs best
  • Spots patterns in successful deals
  • Flags contacts that bounced or unsubscribed
  • Exports reports in CSV or Google Sheets
Live Dashboard Trend Detection CSV Export Google Sheets Sync
