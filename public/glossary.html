Terminology

Outreach Glossary

Every term you'll come across in AI outreach, cold email, and sales automation — explained simply.

A
AI Agent
AI
An autonomous software program that performs tasks — like finding leads, writing emails, or tracking replies — without requiring manual input for each step.
API Integration
Tech
Connecting two software systems via their Application Programming Interface so they can exchange data and trigger actions automatically.
C
Cold Email
Outreach
An unsolicited email sent to a prospect who has no prior relationship with your business, intended to start a conversation or pitch a product.
CPA (Cost Per Action)
Affiliate
A pricing model where payment is triggered only when a specific user action (sign-up, purchase, download) is completed.
CPL (Cost Per Lead)
Affiliate
A model where advertisers pay a fixed amount for each qualified lead generated, regardless of whether that lead converts into a sale.
CPS (Cost Per Sale)
Affiliate
Commission-based model where payment is made only after a successful sale is completed by the referred customer.
Campaign
Outreach
A coordinated sequence of outreach emails sent to a defined list of contacts, typically including follow-up messages over several days.
D
Deliverability
Email
The ability of an email to successfully reach the recipient's inbox rather than being filtered into spam or blocked entirely.
Drip Sequence
Automation
A series of automated follow-up emails sent at scheduled intervals to nurture a lead who hasn't yet responded.
F
Follow-up
Outreach
A subsequent message sent to a prospect who did not respond to the initial outreach email, typically adding new value or a different angle.
L
Lead
Sales
A potential customer or business that has been identified as a prospect for your product or service, but hasn't yet been contacted or qualified.
Lead Generation
Sales
The process of identifying and attracting potential customers to your business, typically via outreach, ads, content, or AI-powered prospecting tools.
O
Open Rate
Analytics
The percentage of recipients who opened a sent email. A key metric for measuring subject line effectiveness and deliverability.
Outreach
Sales
The act of proactively contacting potential customers or partners via email, LinkedIn, or other channels to initiate a business conversation.
P
Personalisation
Email
Tailoring email content to each individual recipient using their name, company, role, or other specific data to improve relevance and reply rates.
Pipeline
Sales
A visual representation of where prospects are in the sales process, from first contact through to closed deal.
R
Reply Rate
Analytics
The percentage of outreach emails that received a response from the recipient. One of the most important indicators of campaign quality.
W
Warm-up
Email
The process of gradually increasing email sending volume from a new domain or inbox to build sender reputation and avoid spam filters.
Workflow Automation
Automation
Using software to automatically execute a series of tasks — such as sending emails, updating records, or triggering follow-ups — based on defined rules or triggers.
