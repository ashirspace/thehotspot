Glossary for modern outreach.

Definitions of key terms used in cold email, pipeline automation, deliverability, and reply tracking.

Alphabet

A-CAccounts, campaigns, cold outreach.
D-IDeliverability, inbox placement.
P-WPersonalization, reply rate, warm-up.

Cold outreach

The practice of reaching out to potential customers you have not contacted before.

Deliverability

The ability of your email to reach the recipient inbox, not spam.

Inbox placement

Where your email lands: primary inbox, promotions, or spam.

Personalization

Tailoring your message to the recipient context or pain points.

Reply rate

The percentage of recipients who reply to your email.

Sequence

A series of emails sent over time to a prospect.

Warm-up

Gradually increasing email volume to build sender reputation.
