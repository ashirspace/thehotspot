We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

This policy explains how we handle information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the service.

2. Information we collect

We collect account details, campaign content, contact records, usage data, and Google OAuth data when you connect Gmail.

3. How we use information

We use information to provide the product, operate outreach workflows, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

4. Google API services

Google data is used only to provide user-facing Gmail-related functionality and is not sold or used for unrelated advertising.

5. Retention and deletion

You can request deletion of account and campaign data by contacting us.
