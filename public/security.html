Security

Controls for sensitive outreach data.

Security at thehotspot focuses on scoped access, clear session handling, revocation paths, and reducing unnecessary copies of customer data.

TRANSPORT
HTTPS required
GOOGLE ACCESS
OAuth-based and revocable
REPORTS
security@thehotspot.in
01

Access and authentication

thehotspot supports account sessions and Google OAuth-based sign-in. Google access can be revoked from your Google account permissions page at any time.

02

Data handling

We aim to limit stored data to what is needed for account operation, outreach organization, and requested workflows. Sensitive account and contact workflows should use least-privilege access wherever possible.

03

Responsible disclosure

If you believe you found a security issue, email security@thehotspot.in with reproducible details. Please avoid accessing data that is not yours.
